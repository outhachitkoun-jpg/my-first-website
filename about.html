Who We Are

The premier tour operator in Laos since 1989.

We Know Laos Best

Inter Lao Tourism operates mostly in Laos, has Lao ownership, and all our staff are Lao with detailed local knowledge. We know our country and can offer you the best of what it has to offer.

Our experienced Lao guides will be honored to show you their country and introduce and explain to you the mystique and secrets of our fascinating culture. You can visit mysterious Wats and caves, enjoy the scenery, and chat with friendly local people.

Quality Standards

We personally select and inspect all the hotels, restaurants, and services we use throughout Laos to ensure that they meet our high standards.

Professional Transport

We have our own air-conditioned vehicles and our multi-language guides know their country very well, guaranteeing a professional experience.

Customized Travel

We offer set tours from North to South, but we also specialize in tailoring tours to suit your specific requirements for individuals or groups.

Experience Authentic Laos

Known as the "Land of a Million Elephants", Laos offers you the opportunity to travel in luxury or to get off the beaten track for some interesting eco- or wilderness tours.

"We know Laos best" - Our local guides can show you their country, including more than just what the guide books tell you.


We seek hotels with character and a quality that sets them apart from the hum-drum. We also offer eco-tourism experiences exploring our remote forests and caves, including village stays in homes where you can experience Lao life.
