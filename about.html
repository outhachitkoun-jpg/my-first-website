Our Story

Based in the beautiful UNESCO World Heritage town of Luang Prabang, Laos Smile Trip is a team of local experts dedicated to showing you the true heart of Laos. With over a decade of experience, we transform journeys into lifelong memories.

From our family-run Villa Kingkham Riverside to our expert-led jungle tours, we provide a deeply personal touch that only true locals can offer.
