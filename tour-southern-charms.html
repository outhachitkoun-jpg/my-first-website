Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival Pakse & Bolaven Plateau

Arrive in Pakse. Drive to Bolaven Plateau to see Tad Fane and Tad Yuang waterfalls. Visit a coffee plantation.

Day 2: Champasak & Vat Phou

Visit the pre-Angkorian Vat Phou temple. Continue to the 4000 Islands.

Day 3: 4000 Islands Exploration

Boat trip to see iridescent dolphins and Liphi Waterfall.

Day 4: Khone Phapheng & Return

Visit the massive Khone Phapheng Falls. Return to Pakse.

Day 5-6: Leisure & Departure

Relax in Pakse or explore the local markets before your departure.
