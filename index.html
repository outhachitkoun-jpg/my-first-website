Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour or ticket?
Booking is easy! Simply browse our tours, click "View Details", and use the booking form or WhatsApp button. For bus/train tickets, visit our dedicated Tickets page. No deposit is usually required for local day tours.
What is your cancellation policy?
We offer flexible cancellations. For most day tours, you can cancel up to 24 hours in advance at no charge. For multi-day packages and train tickets, specific conditions may apply due to external bookings.
Do I need to pay in advance?
For day tours, we often accept payment on the day of the tour. For train tickets and multi-day packages, partial prepayment may be required to secure your reservations. We accept cash (USD/LAK) and bank transfers.
Are the tours private or group-based?
We offer both! Most of our featured day tours can be booked as private experiences for you and your travel companions. We also organize small group treks for those looking to meet other travelers.
