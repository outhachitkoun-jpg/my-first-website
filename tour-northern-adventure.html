Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Luang Namtha

Transfer to Luang Namtha. Visit the local market and prepare for trekking. Overnight in Luang Namtha.

Day 2-3: Jungle Trekking

2-day trek through the Nam Ha NBA. Sleep in an eco-lodge or village homestay. Learn about jungle survival and local plants. Return to town on Day 3.

Day 4: Travel to Nong Khiaw

Scenic drive and boat trip to Nong Khiaw. Enjoy the sunset over the limestone cliffs.

Day 5: 100 Waterfalls & Departure

Morning hike to the 100 Waterfalls or viewpoint. Transfer to Luang Prabang or next Smile Trip.
