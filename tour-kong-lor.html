Itinerary

08:00 AM - Departure

Pick up from your hotel in Thakhek (or Nahin) and drive through scenic karst landscapes.

10:30 AM - Arrive at Kong Lor

Prepare for the expedition. Life jackets and headlamps provided.

11:00 AM - Into the Darkness

Board the longboat. Ride through the 7.5km underground river, stopping to explore the "Beauty Section" on foot to see magnificent rock formations.

01:00 PM - Village Lunch

Emerge on the other side of the cave into a remote valley. Enjoy a local picnic lunch.

04:30 PM - Return

Boat ride back through the cave and transfer back to your hotel.
