Nong Khiaw: 2-Day Trekking, Camping & Kayaking

Nong Khiaw • 4.6/5 Stars Rating • Adventure & Nature

Tour Overview

✨ Highlights

  • 🔸 Breathtaking Views: Trek to Phakao Peak for panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.
  • 🔸 Night Under Stars: Enjoy a traditional Laotian dinner and sleep under the stars or in a tent.
  • 🔸 Remote Villages: Cruise up the Nam Ou River to the blissful remote village of Muang Ngoi.
  • 🔸 Nam Ou Kayaking: Kayak along the Ou River, taking in views of the jungle and wildlife.
  • 🔸 Stunning Landscapes: Immerse yourself in the extraordinary landscapes of Nong Khiaw over 2 days.

Begin an extraordinary 2-day journey deep into the mountains of northern Laos. Experience the thrilling ascent of Phakao Peak, sleep out beneath the twinkling universe, row across the pristine Nam Ou River, and explore small tribal villages nestled among towering karst hills.

Quick Info

  • Duration: 2 Days, 1 Night
  • Location: Nong Khiaw & Muang Ngoi
  • Rating: 4.6 / 5 (White Rabbit Tours)

What to Bring

👟 Hiking shoes 🧥 Warm clothing 📷 Camera 🔦 Flashlight 🧻 Toiletries ⛺ Tent (provided but flexible)

Know before you go

The tour involves physical activities such as trekking and kayaking. Participants should be prepared for an overnight stay in the mountains. Not suitable for: People with back problems, mobility impairments, heart problems, or respiratory issues.

Full Itinerary

Day 1: Trekking & Camping Adventure

02:00 PM: Depart from Heaven Backpackers Stay & Hostel in Nong Khiaw.

02:15 PM: Begin a 3-hour trek through lush jungle trails to reach 360 Phakao Peak, the highest viewpoint in Nong Khiaw, offering panoramic views of the surrounding limestone mountains.

05:30 PM: Enjoy a beautiful evening watching the sunset over the incredible landscape.

07:00 PM: Indulge in a delicious, freshly prepared Laotian dinner.

Night: Camp activities and overnight stay in a tent or under the pristine starry night.

Day 2: River Crusing, Villages & Kayaking

06:00 AM: Wake up for sunrise and witness breathtaking views over the sea of clouds.

07:30 AM: Enjoy breakfast and coffee before descending back down the mountain.

09:30 AM: Arrive back in Nong Khiaw town.

10:00 AM: Cruise in a traditional long boat for 1.5 hours up the scenic Nam Ou River to the wonderfully remote Muang Ngoi village.

11:30 AM: Explore a historic cave and Phanoi viewpoint. Appreciate views of village life and riverbanks. Free time to explore Muang Ngoi.

01:00 PM: Boat to Sopkong Village (a Khmu farming village). Trek for an hour through rice paddies, jungle, and sloping hills.

02:00 PM: Lunch at the stunning That Mok waterfall. Relax, swim, in natural fresh pools.

03:30 PM: Safety briefing and an introduction to kayaking. Kayak for an hour down the spectacular Ou River.

04:30 PM: Arrive back at Nong Khiaw to conclude the tour.
