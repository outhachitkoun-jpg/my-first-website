Afternoon Kuang Si & Sunset Cruise

5 Hours • 13:00 - 18:30 PM • Waterfalls & Mekong River

Tour Overview

✨ Highlights

  • 🔸 Refresh: Feel the refreshing waters of the Kuang Si Waterfalls on your skin
  • 🔸 Wildlife: Explore the Asiatic Black Bear Rescue Centre and forest trails
  • 🔸 Sunset: Enjoy a sunset cruise on the Mekong River with a drink in hand
  • 🔸 Relax: Relax in the turquoise pools of the Kuang Si Waterfalls
  • 🔸 Comfort: Travel in comfort with hotel pickup and drop-off

Begin your afternoon with a hotel pickup in Luang Prabang. Travel by minivan to the stunning Kuang Si Waterfalls, where you can swim in turquoise pools and visit the Bear Rescue Centre. After exploring the falls, we witness how the water irrigates local rice paddies before concluding the day with a peaceful sunset cruise back to town along the Mekong River.

Quick Info

  • Time: flexible timing for private tours
  • Route: Historical Temples → Royal Palace → Local Markets

Detailed Itinerary

13:00 - 13:40 PM: Hotel Pickup

Pickup from your hotel in Luang Prabang city center. Travel by minivan through the scenic landscape (approx. 50 mins).

14:30 PM: Kuang Si Waterfalls

Arrive and enjoy 2 hours of free time. Swim in the turquoise pools, explore forest trails, and visit the Asiatic Black Bear Rescue Centre.

16:30 PM: Heritage Rice Farm

See how the falls' water irrigates terraced paddies and taste fresh, traditionally pressed sugarcane juice.

17:00 PM: Mekong Sunset Cruise

Board a long-tail boat for a gentle 2-hour journey on the Mekong back towards town. Relax with an included drink as the sun sets over the mountains.

18:30 PM: Arrive back in Luang Prabang

Arrive back at the pier in town. (Optional return by minivan at 17:30 PM is available for those on a tighter schedule).
