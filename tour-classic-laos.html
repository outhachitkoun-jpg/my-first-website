Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Luang Prabang

Welcome to Laos! Airport pickup and transfer to hotel. Evening free to explore the famous Night Market.

Day 2: Luang Prabang Heritage

Morning Alms Giving, Wat Xieng Thong, Royal Palace, and sunset from Mount Phousi.

Day 3: Kuang Si Waterfalls & Pak Ou Caves

Visit the stunning waterfalls for a swim, then take a boat trip on the Mekong to the sacred Pak Ou Caves.

Day 4: Transfer to Vang Vieng

Scenic drive or train ride to Vang Vieng. Afternoon Blue Lagoon visit and optional buggy riding.

Day 5: Vang Vieng Adventure

Kayaking on the Nam Song River and exploring Tham Chang Cave. Transfer to Vientiane in the evening.

Day 6: Vientiane City Tour

Discover the capital: Pha That Luang Stupa, Patuxai Victory Monument, and Wat Sisaket.

Day 7: Departure

Last minute souvenir shopping and transfer to Wattay International Airport for your flight home.
