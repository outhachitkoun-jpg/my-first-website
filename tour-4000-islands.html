Itinerary

Day 1: Pakse to 4000 Islands

Depart Pakse and head south. Visit Khone Phapheng Waterfall. Take a boat to Don Khone Island. Explore Liphi Waterfall and the old French colonial bridge.

Day 2: Dolphins & Vat Phou

Morning boat trip to spot Irrawaddy dolphins near the Cambodian border. Return to the mainland and visit the UNESCO World Heritage site of Vat Phou (Khmer ruins) before returning to Pakse.
