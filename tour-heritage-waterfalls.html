Pak Ou Caves & Kuang Si Falls

9 Hours • Mekong Cruise & Turquoise Waterfalls

Tour Overview

✨ Highlights

  • 🔸 Mekong River: Scenic cruise witnessing rural life and traditional fishermen.
  • 🔸 Pak Ou Caves: Discover thousands of Buddha statues in spiritual limestone caves.
  • 🔸 Whisky Village: Visit Ban Xang Hai to see traditional rice wine making.
  • 🔸 Kuang Si Falls: Swim in the famous turquoise pools and visit the Bear Rescue Center.

Experience the ultimate Luang Prabang combo. This journey traces the lifeblood of Laos along the Mekong River to the sacred Pak Ou Caves, journeys through traditional craft villages, and culminates at the breathtaking Kuang Si Waterfalls.

Quick Info

  • Time: 08:30 AM - 04:30 PM Full Day
  • Route: Pak Ou Caves → Whiskey Village → Kuang Si Falls

⚠️ Suitability & Preparation

• Not suitable for: People prone to seasickness (Mekong cruise can be 1.5 - 2 hours).

• What to bring: Comfortable shoes, swimwear, towel, and a change of clothes.

Itinerary

08:15 AM - 08:45 AM - Hotel Pickup

Start your day with a convenient pickup from your accommodation in Luang Prabang center.

Morning - Mekong Cruise & Caves

Board a traditional boat for a cruise on the Mekong. Stop at Ban Xang Hai (Whisky Village) to see the rice wine process. Continue to the Pak Ou Caves to explore the thousands of Buddha images.

12:30 PM - Buffet Lunch

Enjoy a delicious local buffet lunch at a riverside restaurant/elephant camp area.

Afternoon - Kuang Si Waterfalls

Drive to the breathtaking Kuang Si Waterfalls. Enjoy swimming in the turquoise pools and visit the Bear Rescue Center.

Late Afternoon - Hmong Village & Return

Visit Ban Na Uan, a traditional Hmong village. Arrive back in Luang Prabang between 5:30 PM and 6:00 PM.
