Manifa Elephant Camp & Adventure

Full Day • Ethical Encounter & Waterfall Trek

Tour Overview

✨ Highlights

  • 🔸 Ethical Connection: Walk with elephants in the vast forest at the top of Kuang Si Falls.
  • 🔸 Kuang Si Falls: Explore the cascading turquoise pools and hidden rice paddies.
  • 🔸 Sunset Cruise: Relax on a scenic Mekong boat trip back to Luang Prabang.

Manifa Elephant Camp offers a rare opportunity to connect with Laos’ cultural heritage and majestic elephants in a natural riverside setting. We prioritize ethical coexistence, cultural respect, and environmental sustainability, ensuring the well-being of the elephants through responsible care.

Daily Itinerary

08:30 AM - Departure

Pick up from your hotel in Luang Prabang and drive to the Kuang Si region.

10:00 AM - Elephant Encounter

Meet the elephants in the vast forest at the top of the waterfall. Enjoy a meaningful walk beside them and learn about their lives.

12:30 PM - Lunch & Waterfall

Enjoy a delicious local lunch followed by relaxation or swimming at the Kuang Si turquoise pools.

04:00 PM - Sunset Cruise

Embark on a traditional boat for a scenic cruise down the Mekong River as the sun sets.

06:30 PM - Return

Arrive back in Luang Prabang town center.

Quick Info

  • Duration: Full Day (08:30 - 15:30)
  • Experience: Ethical interaction & Forest walking
