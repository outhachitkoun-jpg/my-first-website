Sustainable Craft

Discover the Magic: Eco-Printing

Nestled in the peaceful Ban Pongvan Village, a unique creative experience awaits.

Eco-printing is a sustainable, chemical-free craft that uses the natural tannins and pigments found in leaves, barks, and flowers to create permanent prints on fabric. Every piece is a 100% unique reflection of the Luang Prabang landscape.

Traditional Techniques

Learn the Art of "Nature on Fabric"

Hammering (Hapazome)

An ancient technique to release vibrant plant pigments directly into natural fibers. Instant magic.

Steaming Method

Bundling botanical elements and steaming them to reveal ethereal, detailed silhouettes of the forest.
