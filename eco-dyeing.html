Eco-Printing & Natural Dyeing

Nature's Magic at Ban Pongvan Village

Discover the Magic of Nature: Eco-Printing Workshops in Luang Prabang

Nestled in the peaceful Ban Pongvan Village, just a short ride from the center of Luang Prabang, a unique creative experience awaits you. At our workshop, we invite travelers to slow down and connect with the lush nature of Laos through the art of Eco-Printing.

What is Eco-Printing?

Eco-printing is a sustainable, chemical-free craft that uses the natural tannins and pigments found in leaves, barks, and flowers to create permanent prints on fabric. Unlike synthetic dyes, every piece made here is a 100% unique reflection of the Luang Prabang landscape.

Learn the Art of "Nature on Fabric"

We offer hands-on classes designed for tourists who want a meaningful, "off-the-beaten-path" cultural experience.

Hammering (Hapazome)

Learn how to carefully hammer fresh plants into fabric to release their vibrant colors instantly.

The Steaming Method

Discover how to bundle and steam fabric to create detailed, ethereal leaf prints that last a lifetime.
