Discover the Magic: Eco-Printing

Nestled in the peaceful Ban Pongvan Village, just a short ride from the center of Luang Prabang, a unique creative experience awaits you.

Eco-printing is a sustainable, chemical-free craft that uses the natural tannins and pigments found in leaves, barks, and flowers to create permanent prints on fabric. Every piece is a 100% unique reflection of the Luang Prabang landscape.

Learn the Art of "Nature on Fabric"

Hands-on classes designed for meaningful cultural connection.

Hammering (Hapazome)

Learn how to carefully hammer fresh plants into fabric to release their vibrant colors instantly.

Steaming Method

Discover how to bundle and steam fabric to create detailed, ethereal leaf prints that last a lifetime.
