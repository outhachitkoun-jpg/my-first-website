Slow Boat Mekong 2-Day Up Cruise

Luang Prabang – Pak Beng – Houay Xay • Experience the Mekong's Charm

Tour Overview

✨ Highlights

  • 🔸 Majestic Mekong: Cruise upstream through the scenic heart of Laos.
  • 🔸 Pak Ou Caves: Explore thousands of Buddha statues in spiritual limestone caves.
  • 🔸 Hill Tribe Village: Visit a local community and learn about traditional life.
  • 🔸 Pak Beng: Experience a peaceful overnight stay in this riverside town.

Experience the Mekong’s charm as you cruise upstream through Laos. This two-day journey offers a slow-paced, immersive way to witness the stunning landscapes and remote communities that line the riverbanks.

Quick Info

  • Duration: 2 Days, 1 Night
  • Route: Houay Xai → Luang Prabang
  • Max People: 15 Guests (Boutique environment)

What to Bring

🧥 Light jacket (for mornings) 👟 Comfortable shoes 🕶️ Sunglasses & Sunblock 📷 Camera & Powerbank 🧴 Mosquito Repellent 🎫 Passport & Lao Visa

Itinerary

Day 1: Luang Prabang to Pak Beng

06:30 AM: Hotel pick-up and transfer to the pier.

07:00 AM: Departure heading upstream along the Mekong.

09:00 AM: Visit Pak Ou Caves, home to over 4,000 Buddha statues.

Lunch: Traditional Lao lunch served on board.

04:30 PM - 05:00 PM: Arrival in Pak Beng. Check into your accommodation.

Day 2: Pak Beng to Houay Xay

07:00 AM: Departure from Pak Beng pier (Takeaway breakfast provided).

09:00 AM: Visit an Ethnic Hill Tribe Village to learn about local life.

12:00 PM: Freshly prepared Lao lunch served on board.

04:30 PM: Arrive in Houay Xay for border crossing or onward travel.
